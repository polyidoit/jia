Research Interests

I have interests in both theoretical physics and mathematics.

Mathematics:

  • Quantum algebra and quantum topology;
  • Topological quantum field theory and Topological string theory;

Physics:

  • Quantum foundations, quantum information and quantum computation theory;
  • Quantum phases, topological order;
  • AdS/CFT duality.
